Interaction in the database:

The Encyclopedia Database is designed as an interactive platform for scholars all around the world, to participate actively in research on the material. Commenting and annotation functions are being developed, and will be available for wide use soon.


In case of any urgent matters, wishe to use materials, or participate in the project, please feel free to contact the project team (see contact).
