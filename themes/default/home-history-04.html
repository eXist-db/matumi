Proof-reading, basic TEI mark-up

Automatic text recognition is not sufficient to insert a text “into the database“. Proofreading is necessary for every single text, and the time needed for this step depends very much on the quality of the scan, as well as on the correct settings of the OCR programme.

Basic mark-up has to be inserted according to a certain standard, which makes digital texts interchangable between different projects and platforms. In the Encyclopedia Database, the standard mark-up of the Text Encoding Initiative (TEI) is used to store certain information in the XML document. This includes basic text-specific mark-up as of paragraphs, lists, highlightings, start and end of encyclopedia entries etc., as well as content-specific mark-up for storing information connected to the aims of research.
