Processing and linking of images and page scans

The database displays scans of original pages next to the tagged full text. This allows users to combine their reading of original material with the conveniences of full text search. This feature also helps the editors of the database to easily do corrections and document-specific tagging of the digitized text during the input of new data into the XML files.

Another starting point for research is the analysis of images that come with the encyclopedic text. Images and advertisements may give hints on the intended readership of the books, and give insight into the interpretation of certain topics by the different authors and translators. This is extremely helpful for research if ideas are imported from a foreign culture, as is often the case with encyclopedic works during the time under research.
